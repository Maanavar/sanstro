Overview

What timing guidance covers

Vinaadi calculates specific time windows every day based on a combination of panchangam elements and your personal chart signals. The best window is the period with the strongest astrological support for initiating actions. The caution window is the period where the panchangam signals work against important decisions.

These windows are calculated from real inputs — not generated generically. Two people with different birth charts will have different timing windows on the same day.

  • Panchangam windows

    Rahu Kalam, Yamagandam, Gulika Kalam are marked as caution periods. Amrit Kalam and Abhijit Muhurta are best-window candidates.

  • Dasha period quality

    The current dasha and bhukti lord's strength in your natal chart affects the overall day tone and window quality.

  • Moon nakshatra timing

    Certain nakshatras favour different types of actions. Vinaadi tracks the Moon's daily nakshatra passage and its implications.

  • Transit aspects to natal chart

    If Saturn or Rahu transits a sensitive house for you, that modifies the timing recommendations for that period.

Everyday and significant

What kinds of decisions benefit from timing

Traditional Tamil muhurtha covers a wide range of life events — marriages, housewarmings, business launches, journeys, medical procedures, and important communications. Vinaadi's daily windows are practical guidance for this same range of everyday and significant decisions.

Vinaadi does not claim that a “bad window” guarantees a bad outcome. It provides the astrological context — and lets you decide how to use it. The goal is clarity and calm awareness, not fear-based avoidance.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Are the timing windows the same for everyone?

No. While the panchangam elements (Rahu Kalam, Yamagandam) are the same for a given location, the dasha and transit modifiers are personal. Two people can have different best windows on the same day.

How precise are the time windows?

Windows are calculated to the minute based on Thirukanitham planetary positions and panchangam. They reflect the actual ephemeris, not approximate ranges.

Does Vinaadi support muhurtha selection for ceremonies?

The current daily window guidance is the foundation. More specific muhurtha selection for ceremonies like marriages and housewarmings is on the roadmap.

Can I see timing windows for future dates?

Yes. The dashboard allows you to navigate to future dates to see the windows in advance for planning purposes.
