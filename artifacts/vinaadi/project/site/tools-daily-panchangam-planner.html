The five limbs of the panchangam

Panchangam (பஞ்சாங்கம்) means “five limbs” — the five daily elements that define the quality of each day in the Tamil calendar. Vinaadi computes a precise panchangam from Thirukanitham astronomical data for any date and location.

  • Tithi

    The 30 lunar day phases. Each has a distinct quality for different types of actions.

  • Vara

    The weekday and its planetary lord — Sunday (Sun), Monday (Moon), etc.

  • Nakshatra

    The Moon's nakshatra for the day — affects activities started on that day.

  • Yoga

    Sun-Moon combination — 27 yogas, some auspicious, some inauspicious.

  • Karana

    Half-tithi periods — 11 karanas for finer daily timing.
